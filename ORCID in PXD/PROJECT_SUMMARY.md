# ORCID in PXD — Project Summary

**Project references:** DTP1092 (current) | ITS1214 (origin)
**Last updated:** 14 April 2026 (post-meeting)

---

## What this project is about

Adding ORCID capture and management into the University's PeopleXD HR system, covering two touchpoints:
- **Onboarding** — prompting new starters to register their ORCID via IT Registration
- **Self-service** — allowing all staff to enter/amend their ORCID at any time

The goal is to surface ORCID as a person identifier alongside SSO, pushing value into the Data Warehouse for research and reporting (REF compliance, open access).

## Why the UDF approach was blocked (Grace Parsons investigation, Jan 2025)

Adding a new UDF + checklist item to PeopleXD onboarding triggers automatic emails to everyone already in scope (4,000+ academics/researchers) with weekly reminders. The only workaround is a full onboarding reset, wiping data for 500+ people. Confirmed in the 14 April meeting: PeopleXD SaaS constraints make custom fields, integrations, and API connections undeliverable.
